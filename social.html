FOSTER’S ideology is more inclined towards serving the society rather than to make profits. With the very objective FOSTER has setup a department iCARE (Institute of Continuous Application of Relevant Education) mainly to impart training to the needy students in relevant education , i.e. Language Training and Soft Skill Training to the under privileged students at no cost. We are not ignoring our primary objective – “To serve the society.”

iCARE is engaged in various social activities, through which it caters its real objective. Apart from offering English Speaking and Personality Development Training to poor and needy candidates at no cost we also have partnered with various MCD and Govt schools in Delhi and outside to train the willing candidates at their school premises only, and that too FREE OF COST. Should your school is out of our scope of partnership; or you may like to refer some such students who can ill afford to pay any fee. You can directly write us on foster@fostertraining.in
