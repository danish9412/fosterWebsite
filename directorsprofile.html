Director's Profile

  • Home

Wing Commander Mansoor Siddiqui (Retd) has served the Indian Air Force for over two decades in the Department of Accounts and Finance and during his tenure he was promoted as the Director. Because of keen interest in Training and Knowledge-sharing, he opted for a pre-mature retirement at the age of 43 and established FOSTER ( A Private Limited Company) to pass on the knowledge based on his versatile experience, which has its roots in commitment, self-discipline, decision-making and patriotism.

Through many regress trainings whilst in IAF (Indian Air Force), he developed a groovy personality and gained exceptional soft skills that he practiced during his course of service to the nation. That his cap is ornamented with many feathers, he can be proudly said as a Perfectionist. His academic qualifications include M.Com, MBA (Finance) and PG Dip in Material Management and various others intensive trainings in Behavioral Science. He is also an expert in Oracle, Data Base administration and ERP. With great skills in Management, Wing Commander Siddiqui also has a great appetite for success. In addition to many appreciation letters from his seniors he also received commendation from ‘Vice Chief of Naval Staff’ as he put his efforts in the conversion of a loss-making organization Air Force Naval Housing Board into a self-sustaining venture by making projects of over thousands of crores successful. He had been closely associated with the educational and career counseling activities while being in Air Force and proved himself as an outrageous Mentor, Monitor and Motivator. One quality that distinct him from others is that he does not believe in mere preaching, but he relays his real time experiences during his Motivational and Personality Development Speeches. He is the visiting faculty of several Management Colleges and successfully sharing his knowledge bank.

With his over 25 years of experience and over 10,000 hrs of training, he has gained expetiese in trainings and popularity in corporate world. Through his speeches he not only adds significant value to an individual but also helps him to understand his actual potential and bona-fide character. His positive ideas with devotion and commitment can be defined as correct gratification. Keen sense of humor, facial expression, impressive body language and the style of motivating the listeners with constant interaction are the salient features of his oratory. Till now there have been countless candidates who have been benefitted through HIM. This is an experience by it self to interact with him in person.
