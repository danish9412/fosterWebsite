  • Train The Trainer



Seeing the needs that are required by a trainer to train the personnel globally with enthusiasm, creativity and effervescency, FOSTER has come up with some of the most innovative, rebellious and influential training programs which are especially designed for the trainers or individuals at the organization who are or will be at a designation in the future where they are required to train the staff, their teams or other members of the team.

Objective:

Train the Trainer course will ensure that your trainers are exposed to the most up-to-date training methodologies, approaches and techniques, providing clear examples and case studies of how these can be implemented in your training environment. With a Train the Trainer course, your trainers will be able to translate their professional knowledge and experience into learnable outcomes for your employees.

Who should attend:

  • Trainers
  • Teachers
  • Coaches
  • HR Personnel
  • Managers
  • Team Leaders

    • Benefits

    Train the Trainer course will provide you with the ability to:

  • Train the employees more effectively
  • Conduct assessments and provide accurate training recommendations

    • Course Content:

    Train the Trainer courses are designed to meet the specific needs of our clients depending on their personal / organisational requirements and existing skills set. A Train the Trainer course includes:

  • Adult Learning Theory
  • Corporate Training Methodology
  • Group training dynamics
  • Training Techniques
  • Conflict management
  • Assessment and Evaluation
  • Argument Handling
  • Organisation and Lesson Planning
