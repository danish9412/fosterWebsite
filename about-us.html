What we are

Foster is one of the prominent service provider which concentrates on the transformation of a mere personality to a demanding individual through its specialized training programs: Institutional Training, Corporate training, School Training, and Placement Services, Foster has solutions to all.

Over a short span of time it has been able to bridge the huge manpower gaps of various reputed companies / organization in almost all the verticals such as IT, ITES, Sales, Marketing, Finance, Engineering, KPO, BPO, LPO, HRO, Mass Communication etc. FOSTER Placement Services is striving towards becoming an HR prodigy by providing best of the services to its clientele through its Human Assets.

Foster is nurturing its training services, to groom the today's youth into a prospective workforce by imparting trainings in MBA and Engineering Colleges. From English to Foreign Languages, Literary to Business Writing, Confidence Building to Personality Grooming, the name says it all, our innovation not only lies in the recognition of the Relevant Education for an individual, school or organisation but also the Continuous Application of completely unique training programs for that unique audience to get them the desired results. An initiative of Wing Commander M A Siddiqui (Retd), FOSTER has been able to groom numerous learners in just a span of few years of its inception. Without any external funding FOSTER thru its venture is also fulfilling its social responsibility by providing Free Education to economically backward youth. Also no Placement Fee is charged from the candidates.

What makes us stand out is our potential not only to train the generation but to make them pliable with ever changing Business Environment.


Mission

Wing Commander M A Siddiqui(Retd) has been closely associated with training , human resource ,education and counseling while being in Indian Air Force for over two decades and he developed expertise and passion for training . With the very objective to impart education in the “relevant field” to the youth and provide them suitable avenues, he opted for premature retirement at the age of 43 and established a Private Limited Company just to disseminate the knowledge and provide guidance to the needy ones. He is an educationist, philanthropist and patriotic citizen working seriously to meet the following missions:-

  • To assist students identify placement opportunities
  • To explore the possibilities of employment for them
  • To provide guidance and training to the needy fresher and experienced ones
  • To bridge the gap between the job seekers and job providers
  • To make college student employable in the relevant industry
  • To undertake Corporate and Institutional trainings
